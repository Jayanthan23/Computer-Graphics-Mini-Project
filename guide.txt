

Rotate the Cube:

o key: U' Move (rotate the top layer clockwise)
p key: U Move (rotate the top layer counter-clockwise)
r key: D Move (rotate the bottom layer clockwise)
v key: M Move (rotate the middle layer clockwise)
c key: M' Move (rotate the middle layer counter-clockwise)
t key: Z Move (rotate the entire cube clockwise)
w key: X' Move (rotate the entire cube counter-clockwise)
s key: Sexy Move (perform a specific sequence of moves)

Change Camera View:

g key: Change camera view (rotate the cube 20 degrees around the y-axis)
f key: Change camera view (rotate the cube 20 degrees around the y-axis in the opposite direction)
d key: Change camera view (rotate the cube 90 degrees around the x-axis)

Shuffle the Cube:
a key: Shuffle the cube (perform a random sequence of moves)

Solve the Cube:
q key: Start solving the cube (initiate the solving algorithm)

Additional Keys:
Esc key: Quit the Pygame application
